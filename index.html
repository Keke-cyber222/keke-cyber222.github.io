About me

Designing learning that people love

I hold a Postgraduate Diploma in Business Management (Business Administration) from Wits Business School and a Bachelor of Education in Psychology from the University of the Witwatersrand.

Reliable and self-motivated, I bridge Learning & Development with data analytics — designing engaging training and coaching programmes, then turning learning and business data into actionable insight through SQL, Power BI, data storytelling and visualisation, backed by root cause analysis, critical thinking and risk reporting.

Currently a Learning & Development Coordinator, I build training programmes around Artificial Intelligence, business systems and digital tools, manage a Learning Academy end-to-end, and design SOPs that keep knowledge flowing. With earlier experience at PRP Solutions, Discovery and Wits, I pair instructional design with data skills — SQL, Power BI and data storytelling — to prove that learning works.
